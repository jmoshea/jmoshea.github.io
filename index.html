COVID Vaccination Rates Across the US


Our project aims to clearly show vaccination or immunization rates across the United States. The majority of news media programs and publications show biased and partial data aimed towards a political goal. We hope to clearly, and without bias, show which states have effectively immunized their population. Users can use this information to make informed decisions for travel and safety.



Interesting Details

  • The states with higher vaccination rates have historically supported the Democratic Party. Conversely, states with lower vaccination rates have historically supported the Republican Party.

  • Leading epidemiologists at the World Health Organization estimate that it takes 70% immunization to achieve herd immunity. As expressed in the data, the United States achieved herd immunity in August, 2021 (70% of individuals eligible to get the vaccine at that time).

  • States that have historically mandated mask usage among civilians and workers have higher vaccination rates than states without.

We would like to thank Our World in Data for access to their dataset. Our project examines and provides a visual representation of vaccination rates and total vaccinations across the United States at both a country and state level.
